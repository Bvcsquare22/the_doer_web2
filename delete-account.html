How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Doer account and all associated data using one of the following methods:

1

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

Open the Doer app → Profile → Settings → Delete Account → Confirm deletion.

2

Email Request

Send an email to privacy@thedoer.app with the subject "Delete My Account" and include your registered email address.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days:

Data TypeStatus
Profile information (name, email, photo)Deleted
Fitness activity data (steps, runs, workouts)Deleted
Challenge participation and progressDeleted
Group memberships and messagesDeleted
Location and route historyDeleted
Badges and achievementsDeleted
App preferences and settingsDeleted

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion:

Data TypeRetention PeriodReason
Transaction records7 yearsLegal / tax requirements
Anonymized analyticsIndefiniteCannot be linked to you
Support ticket history90 daysQuality assurance

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose all your fitness history, achievements, and challenge progress. If you rejoin Doer, you will start with a fresh account.

Request Deletion

Send your deletion request directly to:

privacy@thedoer.app
Subject: Delete My Account

Hi,

I would like to delete my Doer account.

Registered email: [your email]

Thank you.

We will process your request within 30 days and send confirmation to your email.
